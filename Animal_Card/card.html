American Pit Bull Terrier

Pit Bulls are among the most affectionate, trustworthy and loyal dogs.

  • Smile: Full of joy and known for their smiles
  • Protective: Known as "nanny dogs" because they were so trusted with children
  • Emotional: Pit Bulls have the largest range of emotions of any dog in the world
  • Photogenic: Featured on the cover of Life Magazine three times - more than any other dog

Pit Bulls are highly a misunderstood dog breed. Contrary to popular belief, these dogs are among the most affectionate and lovable breeds. Historically, Pit Bulls were American Heroes, serving alongside soldiers during World War I and II, and being featured as the US Army mascot on recruiting posters to characterize the United States loyalty, determination and bravery.They also make wonderful therapy, law enforcement, search and resuce dogs as well as amazing family pets that love to cuddle, play and lay in your lap. Irresponsible dog ownership and negative media attention has since tarnished the reputation of a breed formerly considered "America's Dog".
